FAQ

How do I find a hymn quickly?
- Use the search box to search by title.
- Use the number box to jump to a hymn number.

How do favorites work?
- Open a hymn and tap the heart icon.
- Favorites are stored on your device.

What are categories?
- Categories group hymns by theme (for example: worship, praise, gospel, salvation, history).

How do I change the text size?
- Open Settings and choose a font size.

Is the app offline?
- Yes. The app works without internet access once installed.
