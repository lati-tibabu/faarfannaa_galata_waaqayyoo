Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-11

Faarfannaa Galata Waaqayyoo is an offline-first hymn library.

What the app stores on your device
- Favorites: hymn numbers you have marked as favorites.
- Settings: dark mode and font size.
- History: recently viewed hymns.
- Collections: any custom collections you create.

What the app sends off your device
- By default, nothing. The app does not require an internet connection to browse hymns.

Permissions
- The app does not request special permissions for core usage.

Data deletion
- You can clear data by uninstalling the app or by using the Backup & Restore feature to overwrite local data.
